CoMBO: Conflict Mitigation via Branched Optimization for Class Incremental Segmentation

CVPR2025

Kai Fang, Anqi Zhang, Guangyu Gao, Jianbo Jiao, Chi Harold Liu, Yunchao Wei

Class Incremental Segmentation (CIS) poses a significant challenge in balancing the retention of old knowledge while integrating new classes. To address this, we propose CoMBO (Conflict Mitigation via Branched Optimization), a novel approach designed to reduce conflicts in incremental learning.
