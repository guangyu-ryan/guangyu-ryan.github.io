Software Theory and Engineering （Autumn 2024, Graduate Students）

通过本课程的学习，提高学生在软件设计过程中的分析问题与解决问题的能力，掌握高级软件工程理论及工程开发方法，能够有效地策划和管理软件开发活动。同时，通过对前沿技术学习与项目实践，理解云计算、大数据及深度学习等热门领域的软件理论与工程开发过程和方法。

Software Service Engineering (Spring 2024, International Graduate Students)

Software Service Engineering focuses on the provider-client interaction that creates value and shares risks in the software domain. Key topics include: 1) definitions of service and service engineering; 2) Service Engineering Methodology; 3) Service Mode Innovation; 4) Service Modeling; 5) Service System Construction and Operation; 6) Value of Service.
