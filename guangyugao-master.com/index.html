Welcome to Guangyu Gao's HomePage

Guangyu (Ryan) Gao (高广宇)

Associate Professor, in Linc (Lab of INtelligent Computing),

School of Computer Science and Technology, Beijing Institute of Technology

from Oct. 2013, working with Chi Harold Liu.

  • Deputy Director, Institute of Data Science and Knowledge Engineering, BIT（2018-now）
  • Guest Asst. Director, Bureau of Economy and Inform., Shunyi District, Beijing. (2019.1-2020.2)
  • I got my Ph.D. degree on Computer Science and Technology from Beijing University of Posts and
    Telecommunications (BUPT) in 2013, supervised by Huadong Ma.
  • I also spent about one year at National University of Singapore as a government-sponsored
    Joint-Ph.D. student from 2012 to 2013, supervised by Shuicheng Yan.



Research Interests:

Computer Vision with Deep Learning (i.e., Class Incremental Semantic Segmentation, Few Shot Semantic Segmentation, Person Search,
Small Object Detection, Action Recognition and Adversarial Example etc.),
Multimedia Computing (i.e. Cross-Modality Embedding, Multimedia Analysis) etc.
